I am Gabriel Moreau, currently a first-year student at the University Institute of Technology in Montbéliard studying Multimedia and Internet Professions (MMI). 

This project was created as part of an educational exercise within the MMI department at Montbéliard.

I created this website about Avatar because I'm a huge fan of the film, and I wanted to share this universe with others. I aimed to create a site that introduces this world to people who have never seen the movie, while also evoking good memories for those who have seen it, or introducing them to new aspects of this universe.
